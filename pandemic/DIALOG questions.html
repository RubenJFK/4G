👩🏻🎤🧑‍🎨 An interview with VINCENT VAN GOGH

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: Hello, Mr. Van Gogh! Thank you for joining me today. How are you?

🧑‍🎨 VAN GOGH: Hello! I'm happy to talk with you. I feel a little shy, but I’ll do my best.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: Your paintings are so beautiful! Why do you paint so many sunflowers?

🧑‍🎨🌻 VAN GOGH: Thank you! I love sunflowers because they are bright and full of life. They make me happy, even on sad days.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: That’s wonderful! Do you like painting outside?

🧑‍🎨🏞️ VAN GOGH: Yes, I do! Nature is full of colors. I love the sky, the fields, and the trees.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: If you could paint something in Asia, what would it be?

🧑‍🎨🌸 VAN GOGH: I would love to paint cherry blossoms! They are so delicate and peaceful. Have you seen them?

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: Yes, they’re very beautiful! What advice would you give to kids who love to draw and paint?

🧑‍🎨🖌️ VAN GOGH: Never stop creating! Use your imagination and practice every day. Art is a way to show the world your feelings.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: That’s great advice! What do you do when you feel sad or stuck?

🧑‍🎨🎨 VAN GOGH: I paint! Even when I feel sad, painting helps me feel better. I think about colors and try to make something beautiful.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: That’s a good idea. What’s your favorite painting that you’ve made?

🧑‍🎨🌌 VAN GOGH: I love Starry Night. It shows how I feel about the night sky—so big, full of wonder, and full of dreams.

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: That’s such a beautiful painting! If you could say one thing to people who love your art, what would it be?

🧑‍🎨😊 VAN GOGH: I would say thank you! It makes me happy to know my art brings joy to others. Keep enjoying art!

👩🏻🎤 INTERVIEWER: Thank you so much, Mr. Van Gogh! Your art inspires so many people, including me!

🧑‍🎨😊 VAN GOGH: Thank you for your kind words. Keep enjoying art and sharing it with others!
